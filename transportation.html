Traveling in Taniti

Easy ways to arrive and explore the island.

Arriving on the island

Air

Nearly all visitors arrive by air. A small, efficient airport for jets and propeller planes serves the island.

Ship

A small cruise ship docks once a week at Yellow Leaf Bay for an overnight stay, offering a glimpse of island life.

Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport in order to welcome larger aircrafts in the coming years.

Getting Around

Private Transportation

Taxi: Available in Taniti city.
Rental Car: Contact local agency near the airport.
Bus: Contact us for rental options.
Bike: Available for rent from several vendors.

Public Transportation

Frequent buses run through Taniti City
daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.

Bike helmets are required by law.

Walking

Taniti City and Merriton Landing are both flat, safe, and walkable.
Perfect for more casual exploration.
